We believe men’s haircuts are NOT one size ﬁts all. We are the anti-barber. No clippers. We are 100% scissor over comb which means every haircut we give is tailored to the individual, taking into consideration hair texture, hair direction, face structure/shape, personal style and desired look to achieve a result most men didn’t even know was possible for their hair. Fleischman Flow means having a haircut that grows out beautifully so it looks just as good on day 60 as it did on day 1. No one has time to get a haircut every two weeks because their hair sticks up in weird places a week after a haircut or because it’s a struggle to style. Our clients can go up to 4 – 8 weeks between haircuts and love the way their hair looks at every stage.
